— Why Choose Us

Corpus Christi's Local Experts You Can Actually Trust

Corpus Christi's combination of intense UV, salt air, and extreme humidity is brutally hard on exterior paint. Coastal Coat uses high-grade weatherproofing primers and top coats specifically selected for our climate — the difference in longevity is remarkable.

Inside, we are obsessive about the details. We sand every surface, tape every edge, and spray cabinet doors in our shop to achieve a factory-smooth finish. The prep work takes half the project time — and it shows in the result.
